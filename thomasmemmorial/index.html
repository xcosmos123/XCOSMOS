A Life of Faith & Service

Kottayam, Kerala Beloved Husband

Thomas Joseph was a beacon of light in his community. Born in the lush landscapes of Kottayam, he dedicated his life to his family, his farm, and his faith. Known for his gentle smile and unwavering integrity, Thomas served as a pillar of St. Mary's Church for over 40 years.
